Dale Hollow Community College

Our Mission

Dale Hollow Community College is a public, comprehensive community college offering quality, innovative educational programs, support, and services. We are committed to building partnerships, strengthening internal and external community engagement, and promoting diversity, cultural awareness, and economic development to prepare students for successful careers, university transfer, and meaningful civic participation in a global society.

Admissions

We serve students from all walks of life. Whether you are currently in high school, have been out of school for many years, are looking to transfer, or want to take some classes for personal enrichment, we look forward to welcoming you as a member of the Dale Hollow Family. If you have questions about program offerings or the admissions process, stop by the office or contact us at (555) 555-5555 or chat with us during office hours. No appointment necessary!
